How Our AI-Powered Cancer Screening System Works

1

Collect Patient Data

Our system begins by collecting essential patient information, including imaging data (Histopathology & Dermioscopic Images) to create a comprehensive health profile.

2

AI-Driven Imaging Analysis

Advanced machine learning algorithms analyze the imaging data for potential signs of cancer. Each AI model is specifically trained to identify particular types of cancer, such as breast or skin cancer, ensuring precision and accuracy in detection

3

Cancer Detection & Prioritization

Based on the imaging analysis and patient data, the system identifies potential areas of concern and helps prioritize cases that require immediate attention, enabling timely intervention

4

Reporting & Insights

Our system generates detailed reports for healthcare providers, offering clear interpretations and actionable insights that support informed decision-making.
